Andhika N. (6 Feb 2025)

Problem 1. A 20 year bond matures for its par value of 10,000. The coupon payable semi-annually is 400. Calculate the price of the bond at a 6% yield rate convertible semi-annually.

Solution 1. Diketahui:

  • F = 10,000

  • Kupon Semi-annual (semesteran) sebesar 400 ΓÇäΓƒ╣ΓÇäFΓÇà├ùΓÇàrΓÇä=ΓÇä400

  • yield rate convertible semi annually ΓÇäΓƒ╣ΓÇäi(2)ΓÇä=ΓÇä6%Γƒ╣ yield rate efektif semesteran (dinotasikan i di jawaban ini): \(i = \frac{i^{(2)}}{2} = 3\% = 0.03\)

  • 20 tahun dengan kupon semesteran ΓÇäΓƒ╣ΓÇänΓÇä=ΓÇä20ΓÇà├ùΓÇà2ΓÇä=ΓÇä40

  • C = F = 10,000 (tidak diberitahu, berarti asumsikan sama dengan Face Value)

Ditanya: Harga dari obligasi
Jawab:
\[\begin{aligned} P &amp;= F \times r \times \ax{\angl{n}i} + C \times v_{i}^{n}\\ &amp;= (F \times r) \times \ax{\angl{40}3\%} + C \times v_{3\%}^{40}\\ &amp;= 400 \times \frac{1-v^{40}}{i} + 10,000 \times v_{3\%}^{40}\\ &amp;= 400 \times \frac{1-(1.03)^{-40}}{0.03} + 10,000 \times {(1.03)}^{-40}\\ &amp;=12,311.4772 \approx 12,311.48 \end{aligned}\]
